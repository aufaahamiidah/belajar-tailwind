Seventeen

Seventeen (Hangul: 세븐틴; written as SEVENTEEN or SVT) is a South Korean boy band formed by Pledis Entertainment. The 13-member group is divided based on their specialties into 3 sub-units; hip-hop unit, vocal unit, and performance unit.
