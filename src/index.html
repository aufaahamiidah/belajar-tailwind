Say The Name!

Seventeen

SEVENTEEN is a mega group with 13 members and 3 units, meaning that they form a team. Preparing for their debut with a differentiated promotion that introduced a pre-debut system and opened up their pre-debut training process to the public, SEVENTEEN made their debut in May 2015 with their first mini album “17 CARAT” and quickly became super rookies. Three different units (hip-hop team, vocal team, and performance team) coexist in the group, and each team participates in the overall production of the album, living up to the label of self-produced idols. Through music and performances that only SEVENTEEN can show, the group has gone from being labeled as super rookies, self-produced idols, and young idols immediately after their debut, to now being the biggest idols in Korea and abroad, further solidifying their position in the music industry.
